Ingredients

  • Cheese of your liking
  • Potatoes of your liking
  • Bacon bits
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Slow roast taters to appropriate temperature
  2. Shred block of cheese to prefered style
  3. Pull taters half-way through cooking and place in baking dish
  4. mix in taters with cheese and bacon bits accordingly
  5. put it oven at 350 degrees to bake for 20 minutes
  6. add parsley and let cool for 3 minutes
  7. Enjoy
