Taco Salad

The combination of beef, tomatoes, corn, black beans, avoacado, onions, and dressing make for a lovely dish any time!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Corn
  • Black Beans
  • avoacado
  • Onions
  • Catalina Dressing
  • Bag of Doritos

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef to an appropriate temperature
  2. chop lettuce, corn, black beans, avoacado, and onions
  3. Place step 2 ingredients in mixing bowl
  4. crush up bag of Doritos and dump into bowl from step 3
  5. Mix in cooked ground beef into mixture
  6. Use 1/2 bottle of catalina and stir the bowl
  7. Mix in the rest of the bottle of Catalina
  8. Stir and toss until desired distribution
